Rui Bruno Santos

Rui Bruno Santos is a PhD candidate in Communication Sciences at ISCTE – University Institute of Lisbon, where his research examines how communication can contribute to the prevention of tax non-compliance. His research focuses on how communication shapes social norms around taxation and on the relationship between tax compliance and citizens’ responses to fiscal policy in digital contexts. Since 2023, he has been developing computational approaches for social media analysis, combining R, Python, and advanced content-analytics techniques. His work has appeared in a number of journals including the Online Information Review, Journal of Media Ethics, and Observatorio.
He holds an MSc in Management and a BA in Marketing and Communication from Universidade Atlântica, Portugal.

Code

In my spare time, I enjoy building Python scripts. Some of them are useful, some of them are more specific to research projects.

TheGuardian-script-for-researchers Version 1.0

This project enables the extraction of articles from The Guardian newspaper using the API provided by the newspaper, and saves them into a PDF file.

DOI / Repository: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14505940

Reddit-extract-comment Version 1.0

This project enables the extraction of comments from Reddit platform using the API provided by them, and saves them into a CSV file.

DOI / Repository: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14505944

All projects are open-source and available on Zenodo. Please cite if it is useful for you.
